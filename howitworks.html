How ElectroTide Works

ElectroTide makes it easy for boat owners to access clean, renewable energy right at the dock. Our system combines compact tidal turbines with smart, accessible charging infrastructure to simplify electric boating and reduce environmental impact.

From Tides to Tech

Everything begins with the ocean. Our floating horizontal-axis underwater turbines are placed just off the coast in areas with consistent tidal movement. These turbines spin with the natural current, converting kinetic energy from the tides into clean electricity 24/7.

This electricity is delivered directly to on-shore charging docks through durable, marine-grade underwater cables. Energy is either used immediately or stored in a nearby battery system, so users can charge their boats even when the tide is low or during peak demand.

Charging Made Easy

Once docked, boat owners simply plug into a charger—just like electric vehicle drivers do on land. Our docks use standardized NACS connectors, making them compatible with most electric boats currently on the market. Each charger is equipped with smart technology to manage energy flow and track usage.

Marinas equipped with ElectroTide chargers offer both pay-per-use and discounted membership options. Members can enjoy lower rates, priority access at busy docks, and reward points with every charge.

Why It Matters

  • Green Power: No fossil fuels, no noise. Just clean energy from the tides
  • Marine-Safe: Quiet motors reduce underwater noise pollution, protecting whales and marine life
  • Job Creation: Our installations support local employment in engineering, construction, and maintenance
  • Community Partnership: We aim to collaborate with Indigenous coastal communities to support sustainable marine stewardship and clean energy access
  • Scalable Infrastructure: The system is designed for replication—starting with small docks, expanding to marinas, ferries, and ports
